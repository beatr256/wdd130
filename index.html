Uganda

Uganda is in East Africa, landlocked; bordered by Kenya, South Sudan, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, and Tanzania. Known as the “Pearl of Africa,” Uganda has diverse landscapes, including Lake Victoria (the world’s second-largest freshwater lake), the River Nile (the world’s longest river), tropical forests, savannahs, and mountain ranges like the Rwenzori (“Mountains of the Moon”).
