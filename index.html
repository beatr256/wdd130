Uganda

Uganda is in East Africa, landlocked; bordered by Kenya, South Sudan, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, and Tanzania. Known as the “Pearl of Africa,” Uganda has diverse landscapes, including Lake Victoria, the River Nile, tropical forests, savannahs, and mountain ranges like the Rwenzori Mountains (“Mountains of the Moon”).
